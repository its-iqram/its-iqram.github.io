## Key Insights
- **Air resistance**: $F = -bv$ where $b$ is the drag coefficient
- **Terminal velocity**: $v_t = \sqrt{\frac{mg}{k}}$ for quadratic drag
- **Energy loss**: Proportional to $v^2$ in fluid dynamics

## Problems Solved
1. **Projectile with linear drag**: Solved $\frac{dv}{dt} = -g - \frac{b}{m}v$
2. **Oscillatory motion**: Analyzed $\frac{d^2x}{dt^2} + \omega^2x = 0$
3. **Damped oscillator**: $\frac{d^2x}{dt^2} + 2\beta\frac{dx}{dt} + \omega_0^2x = 0$
